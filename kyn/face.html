ABOUT THE IDEA

I came in Boston around August first week and started living in a big 22-storey city view apartment, being a student I was always attracted by word 'FREE' , I came to know that in Boston there is a concept called "Steal The Trash", where people get rid of their old stuff and needy ones pick it up.

I wanted a study table, I waited for about a month for the event and finally got a heavy study table. I was very happy that I got one table after so much of search on n number of streets, just when I was busy bragging about me, I saw my neighbor dumping a brand new study table outside, I could not believe my luck!!

So I thought had I been connected to my neighbor ,my life would have been much easier. Also I genuinely believe that one may be connected to world via FACEBOOK,TWITTER...but the fact is that people near you matter a lot and those are YOUR NEIGHBOURS(however good or bad they are....u need to know them). THAT GAVE BIRTH TO THIS IDEA .
